Goulash

Description:

An easy dinnertime recipe that takes only about 20 minutes to make.Using a few ingredients to make a hearty meal for the family after a long day at WORK.

Once you use this recipe you will add it to your weekly dinner list.

Ingredients:

  • Tri Color Rotini
  • Canned Stewed Tomatoes (12 oz)
  • 1 lbs. Hamburger meat
  • Garlic and Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil a pot of water for the Rotini.
  2. While the Rotini is cooking brown the hamburger meat in a skillet.
  3. Once the hamburger meat is browned, set it aside.
  4. Strain the Rotini, place back in pot.
  5. Add the browned meat, can of stewed tomatoes, and salt and garlic to the pot of Rotini.
  6. Stir to combine then serve with buttered bread and Parmesean cheese.
